Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

ZaksClips ("the Application") is a desktop tool that helps content creators automate the clip workflow for streaming platforms. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Application accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Access

ZaksClips connects to third-party platforms on your behalf using OAuth 2.0. The following data may be accessed:

  • TikTok: Access to upload videos to your TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API. We access only the scopes required to post video content. We do not read your TikTok followers, messages, or profile beyond what is necessary to complete the upload.
  • YouTube: Access to upload videos to your YouTube channel via the YouTube Data API v3. We access only the scopes required to upload and manage your own video content.
  • Twitch: Read-only access to your Twitch clips and VOD data via the Twitch Helix API. We do not modify your Twitch account in any way.

2. How Data Is Stored

  • All data processed by ZaksClips remains on your local machine. No data is transmitted to any ZaksClips server.
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens for TikTok, YouTube, and Twitch are stored locally in token files on your device. These tokens are never shared with third parties.
  • API credentials (client keys and secrets) are stored in a local .env file on your device.
  • Video files and metadata are stored locally in your application output directory.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over retention and deletion:

  • You can revoke OAuth tokens at any time by disconnecting ZaksClips from your TikTok, YouTube, or Twitch account settings on each respective platform.
  • Local token files can be deleted at any time by removing the corresponding files from the application directory.
  • Video files and metadata can be deleted through the application interface or directly from your file system.

4. Third-Party Services

ZaksClips integrates with the following third-party platforms. Your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Twitch Privacy Notice

ZaksClips also optionally uses the Anthropic Claude API for AI-generated titles and captions. If enabled, clip transcripts are sent to Anthropic for processing. See the Anthropic Privacy Policy.

5. No Analytics or Tracking

ZaksClips does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising networks, or any form of tracking. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

ZaksClips is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children.

7. Your Rights

Because ZaksClips does not collect or store your data on any external server, there is no user account or centralized data store to request access to or deletion of. All data exists locally on your own device under your control. To revoke platform access, use the account settings of each respective platform.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
